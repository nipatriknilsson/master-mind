How to play Mastermind

The program selects four pegs and hides them for you. Your job is to guess them.

Start guessing by moving the mouse pointer to a peg button, left click and select a color. Repeat that four times and press check.

The program gives you feedback with black or white squares. A black one means that one peg is correct. White means the color exists, but is wrongly placed.

Black and White color codes does not reveal the position of which peg is right or wrong.

Options

All options takes effect next time you select menu Game->New.
